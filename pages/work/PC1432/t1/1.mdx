# 1

The shared distance between all four points to the centre:
```
\begin{align*}
r &= \sqrt{\left(\df{1}{2} a\right)^2 + \left(\df{1}{2} a\right)^2} \\
  &= \sqrt{\df{1}{2} a^2} \\
  &= \df{a}{\sqrt{2}}
\end{align*}
```

Electric field due to the top-left charge (towards bottom-right):
```
\begin{align*}
E &= \df{kq}{\f{1}{2}a^2} \\
  &= \df{2kq}{a^2}
\end{align*}
```

Net direction: down (clearly)

Net magnitude:
```
\begin{align*}
|E| &= \df{1}{\sqrt{2}} \times 4 E \\
    &= \df{1}{\sqrt{2}} \times 4 E \\
    &= \df{8}{\sqrt{2}} \df{kq}{a^2} \\
    &= \df{2}{\sqrt{2} \pi\epsilon_0} \df{q}{a^2} \\
    &= \df{\sqrt{2}q}{\pi\epsilon_0 a^2}
\end{align*}
```
