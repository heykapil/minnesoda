Relationships
-------------

![](/physics/ec/relationships.png)

- `\vec{F}`: [Electric force][f] between two charges `q_1` and `q_2` that
  are `\vec{r}` apart.
- `\vec{E}`: [Electric field][e] at a point `\vec{r}` away from a charge
  `q`.
- `U`: [Electric potential energy][u] between two charges `q_1` and `q_2`
  that are `r` apart.
- `V`: [Electric potential][v] at a point `r` away from a charge `q`.

Electric force between two charges (Coulomb's Law)
--------------------------------------------------

```
F = \df{1}{4\pi\epsilon_0}\df{|q_1 q_2|}{r^2}
```

Electric field
--------------

```
\begin{align}
E
&= \df{1}{4\pi\epsilon_0}\df{|q|}{r^2} \\
&= \df{F}{q}
\end{align}
```

Electric potential energy
-------------------------

```
U = \df{1}{4\pi\epsilon_0} \df{q_1q_2}{r}
```

Electric potential
------------------

```
\begin{align}
V
&= \df{1}{4\pi\epsilon_0} \df{q}{r} \\
&= \df{U}{q}
\end{align}
```

Electric Flux
-------------

```
\Phi_E = EA \cos\phi
```

Gauss Law
---------

```
\Phi_E = \df{Q_{\text{encl}}}{\epsilon_0}
```
