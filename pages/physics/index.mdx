# Every formula of PC1432

## Coulomb's Law

$$
F = K\f{\abs{q_1 q_2}}{r^2}
$$

## Electric Field

$$
\begin{align}
\vec{E} &= \lim\limits_{q_0\to 0}\f{\vec{F}}{q_0} \\
F_0 &= K\f{\abs{q q_0}}{r^2} \\
E &= \f{F_0}{q_0} = K\f{\abs{q}}{r^2} \\
\vec{E} &= K\f{q}{r^2}\hat{r}
\end{align}
$$

## Applied: Electric field due to ring charge

$$
\lambda
= \f{\text{Total charge on the ring}}{\text{Length of the ring}}
= \f{Q}{2\pi a}
$$

Length $\d s$ with charge $\d Q$:

$$
\d E = K\f{\lambda \d s}{a^2 + x^2}
$$
