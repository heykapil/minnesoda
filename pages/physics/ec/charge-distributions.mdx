Charge distributions
====================

Linear charge density `\lambda` is charge per unit length
(`\mathrm{Cm^{-1}}`).

Surface charge density `\sigma` is charge per unit area
(`\mathrm{Cm^{-2}}`).

Volume charge density `\rho` is charge per unit volume
(`\mathrm{Cm^{-3}}`).

```
E_{\text{net}} = \int dE
```

Net field of line charge of infinite length
-------------------------------------------

```
E_{\text{net}} = \df{1}{2\pi\epsilon_0}\df{\lambda}{x}
```

where `x` is the distance to the nearest point on the line.
