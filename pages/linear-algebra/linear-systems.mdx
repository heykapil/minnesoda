Linear Systems
==============

Standard Form
-------------

```
\begin{align}
x + y + z &= 1 \\
2x - y + 3z &= 2 \\
x + 2y + 7z &= 5 \\
0x - 6y + 2z &= 0 \\
\end{align}
```

Matrix equation form
--------------------

That is the form `\v{Ax} = \v{b}`, where

```
\v{A} =
\begin{bmatrix}
1 &  1 & 1 \\
2 & -1 & 3 \\
1 &  2 & 7 \\
0 &  6 & 2 \\
\end{bmatrix}

,\;

\v{x}
= \begin{bmatrix}
x \\
y \\
z \\
\end{bmatrix}

,\;

\v{b} =
\begin{bmatrix}
1 \\
2 \\
5 \\
0 \\
\end{bmatrix}
```

`\v{A}`: coefficient matrix  
`\v{x}`: variable matrix  
`\v{b}`: constant matrix

`\v{u}` is said to be a solution to the linear system `\v{Ax} = \v{b}`
if `\v{Au} = \v{b}`.
